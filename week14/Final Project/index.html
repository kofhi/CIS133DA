Learn and preserve the wildlife of Arizona on campus

Explore and discover interesting plants and animals native to Arizona from all around the state, while being able to learn from and connect with professionals, enthusiasts, as well as like-minded learners through different projects and activities, all at Scottsdale Community College.

What is the Sonoran Desert Club?

An organization under the Center for Nature and Urban Wildlife Department that provides students of any program the opportunity to understand, appreciate, and share their passion of the wildlife, ecology, and biodiversity of the Sonoran Desert to others.

A place that welcomes all talent, where students can partake in different projects and activities based on their skills and preferences that will help educate and promote the flowering fauna.
