Best Web Hosting Providers

Stage your internet presence. Connect with the world.

To get your website up and accessible on the internet, you’ll need to “rent out” a server from a web hosting provider where you can store and maintain your website’s files and applications.

With so many web hosting providers and each having multiple service packages, choosing the right one for you can get overwhelming. Thus, here’s a compilation of three web hosting providers that provide the most essential features at a reasonable price for those wanting to start with publishing websites.

Basic Criteria (Minimum Requirements)

The web hosting providers we will be looking at are considered for meeting the minimum requirements that account for the usability of the services:

  • Supports PHP and MySQL - most favored general-purpose scripting language and database management system for websites, providing great speed and flexibility.
  • Offers e-commerce capabilities - essential for creating safe and secure websites for businesses.
  • Provides at least 1GB disk space - to be able to support a large amount of files and applications in the long run.
